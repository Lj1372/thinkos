💬 Frequently asked questions
What is ThinkOS? ▼
ThinkOS is a thinking companion that helps you break down any decision, problem, or idea using proven mental frameworks — First Principles, Stoicism, Inversion, and 24+ other lenses. It doesn't give you answers; it helps you think more clearly so you arrive at better ones yourself.
What does Pro include? ▼
Pro unlocks all 24+ extended thinking lenses, Pro Council (run any combination of all lenses simultaneously), the Decision Planner, cloud session history synced across devices, session analytics, the weekly thinking report, and priority access to new features as they ship.
Is my data private? ▼
Your thoughts are sent to our AI model to generate responses and are not stored beyond your session unless you're logged in with Pro (where sessions are saved to your account only). We never sell or share your data. You can delete your account and all data at any time.
How does the 7-day free trial work? ▼
Your 7-day Pro trial starts automatically on your first visit — no card required. You get full access to every Pro feature. After 7 days you can upgrade to keep access. If you subscribe, Stripe also gives you a 7-day no-charge period before your first billing.
Can I cancel my subscription? ▼
Yes — cancel any time with no penalty. Go to ⚙ Account in the sidebar and click "Cancel subscription." You keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period and will never be charged again after that.
What is the refund policy? ▼
7-day full refund, no questions asked. If you subscribed in the last 7 days, email ThinkOS137@outlook.com and we'll refund you in full. Accidental charges are always refunded. Outside 7 days, email us and we'll review fairly — we're a small team and we care about being honest. Lifetime plans are non-refundable after 7 days.
How do I delete my account? ▼
Log in, then click ⚙ Account in the bottom of the sidebar. Scroll to "Delete my account" — you'll be asked to confirm by typing DELETE. This immediately cancels your subscription (no further charges), permanently deletes all your sessions, journal entries, and profile data, and removes your login. This cannot be undone.
Which lenses should I start with? ▼
For most decisions, start with REI Council (gut check), then Inversion (what could go wrong), then First Principles (strip back assumptions). For big life questions, try Full Core Council which runs all six core lenses in sequence. Not sure? Use the Suggest a Lens button — it reads your situation and recommends the best two lenses automatically.
